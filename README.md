# WebDev_Project_OtakuHaven

Otaku Haven is a project aimed at merging an anime product store with an engaging community space. It provides users with a platform to shop for anime merchandise, engage in forum-style conversations, and explore new series through a random anime suggestion generator.  

Features  

Anime Product Store: Explore and buy products themed around anime.  

Forum-style Discussion Platform: Connect with other anime enthusiasts in a forum system reminiscent of 4chan.  

Random Anime Suggestion Generator: Receive personalized anime recommendations based on your preferences.  

Vibrant Retro Aesthetic: A lively and distinctive design influenced by anime culture and the store's theme.  

Accessibility Options: Includes fonts suitable for colorblind and dyslexic users, along with text-to-speech functionality.
